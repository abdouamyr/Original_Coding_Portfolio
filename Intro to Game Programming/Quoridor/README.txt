QUORIDOR 

Description: 
Employment of turns. Employment of 2d boolean array to determine valid moves and placements. Use of advanced click controls and a variety of 2d arrays of GameObjects. Dialogue box to provide player feedback, including a coroutine to reset back to default message after a set amount of time. 

Controls/Gameplay: 
Use mouse to select respective pawn (sphere) or wall pieces (at bottom), and either move or place wall pieces. First player to get pawn across to opposite side wins, each turn can either be the placing of two wall segments adjacently or moving the pawn once. Player cannot box in the enemy pawn.
